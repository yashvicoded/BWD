Terms & Conditions

Please read our terms of service before booking a tour

1. Booking and Payments

All bookings are subject to availability. A deposit of 30% is required to secure your reservation. The remaining balance must be paid 15 days prior to the departure date. If the booking is made within 15 days of departure, full payment is required immediately.

2. Cancellations and Refunds

If you wish to cancel your booking, you must notify us in writing. The following cancellation fees apply:

  • Up to 30 days before departure: Full refund less administrative fee.
  • 15-29 days before departure: 50% refund.
  • Less than 15 days before departure: No refund will be issued.

3. Itinerary Changes

While we strive to provide the exact itinerary described, Wanderlust Journeys reserves the right to make changes to the schedule, accommodations, or activities due to weather, local conditions, or unforeseen events to ensure traveler safety.

4. Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to protect your investment. Wanderlust Journeys provides basic insurance, but comprehensive coverage for high-altitude treks and wilderness safaris is the responsibility of the traveler.
