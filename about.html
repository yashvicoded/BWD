Who We Are

Founded in 2012 in the heart of Mumbai, Wanderlust Journeys was born from a singular passion: to show the true magic of India to the world.

We are a team of travel purists who believe that tourism goes beyond ticking checkboxes on an itinerary. Over the last decade, we have grown from a small passionate group of adventurers to one of India’s most trusted boutique travel agencies, successfully arranging travel for over 15,000 happy wanderers across 120+ destinations nationwide.

Whether you seek the tranquil backwaters of Kerala, the stark mystical beauty of Ladakh, the royal history of Rajasthan, or an adrenaline-filled tiger safari in Corbett — we handcrafted your journey with care, expertise, and love.
